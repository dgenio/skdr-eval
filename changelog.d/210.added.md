`evaluate_sklearn_models` gained `execution_mode={"auto","standard","large_data"}` and `chunk_size`. `large_data` builds and predicts the policy-induction feature matrix — the dominant transient allocation on large logs — in row-blocks, bounding peak memory; it is numerically identical to `standard` (parity-tested to bit equality). `auto` switches to the chunked path once the log reaches `LARGE_DATA_ROW_THRESHOLD`.
