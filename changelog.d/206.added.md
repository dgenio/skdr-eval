`evaluate_sklearn_models(..., requires_overlap=True)` runs a cheap positivity/overlap precheck (eligibility match-rate and a single-shot propensity floor) *before* the expensive cross-fitting and bootstrap, raising the new `InsufficientOverlapError` immediately when the logs cannot support off-policy evaluation at all. Off by default; tune via `overlap_floor` / `min_match_rate`. `overlap_floor` and `min_match_rate` are validated up front (must lie in (0, 1] and [0, 1] respectively).
